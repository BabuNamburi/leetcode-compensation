So, there are two types of roles available for you as a Educator.
Part-Time
Full-Time
As a part-Time Employee you are expected to work 4-6 hours a day, Each class is of 45 mins - 1 hour.
As a Full-Time expected time is 6-8 hours.
The compensation is total variable component, you don't have a fixed-amount as salary.
For Demo classes you'll get 50 INR per Class, for Paid class you'll get 250 INR per class.
